From: A.C. Holloway, To: Jeanette Knox Chandler

Mailed From: Pearl, Illinois on December 23, 1927



Mailing Address:

Mrs. E. H. Chandler

2018 Newland Ave.

Chicago, Illinois



Return Address:

After 5 days return to

A.C.. Holloway

Pearl, Illinois



12/23/27

Mrs. E.H. Chandler,

I got your letter and sent your order to Anna yesterday. I will send you a bill of it. But you just wait until you come home to pay.

Thanking you,

A.C. Holloway
